Shaking My Angels

Healing the silence through the S.O.S.

My father was a hidden child—a survivor of the Holocaust who carried his history in silence. That silence created a gap in my own narrative, a tension I spent years trying to resolve through "running" and "angry quits."

This project is about bridging that gap. It is my duty to document the story he could not tell. By applying the Sovereign Operating System to ancestral trauma, I am turning survival into intelligence.
